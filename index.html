Exercise 4: Grammar Spotlight & Detective Notebook

Grammar Rule: Infinitives of Purpose

We use the Infinitive of Purpose (to + base verb) to explain why someone does something or why something exists.

  • Incorrect: Maya woke up early for finding inner peace.
  • Correct: Maya woke up early to find inner peace.

Tip: You can usually replace it with "in order to" in your head to check if it's correct!
